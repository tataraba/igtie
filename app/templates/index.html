FAQ

What is WRPLyst?
WRPLyst is a web application that helps you track the media content you've been missing. Think of it as a goal-setting application without the nag factor. It is designed to motivate you to sepend more time with the things that bring you joy!
How is it different than other goal-setting apps?
Other goal-setting apps are designed to encompass a large number of activities and are weighed down by the broad feature-sets that sometimes don't seem very well connected. WRPLyst is different by focusing specifically on a set of entertainment categories. It brings you closer to books, movies, tv shows, albums, and games that you've been missing. A simple interface and intuitive controls keep you motivated to catch up on (or revisit) the content you crave.
How much does it cost to use?
WRPLyst is completely free for all users. You can register for free and start tracking your goals within minutes. There are enhanced features available for a small monthly fee, but that is entirely optional.
How do I use WRPLyst?
You can start by signing up for a free account. Once you're logged in, you can immediately begin creating yoru WRPLyst. Additionally, if you fill out your profile, you may begin to see suggested lists of titles to add to your lists based on your preferences. Your user data is stored securely and is not shared with any third parties.
