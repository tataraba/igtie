Lists for another day

1

No Time?

Someday is a repository for all those things you want to get to, eventually, but you just cannot quite commit to right now.

2

No Rush!

It is not a tracking app, and will not make you feel guilty for not accomplishing certain goals under some arbitrary time frame.

3

Some day

Instead, it will capture your interests from a wide range of mediums all in one place, without the hassle of setting time frames or schedules.

Features

Group by type

Someday groups lists by type. This will help you choose what to reach for when the proverbial rainy day comes. These are a few of the categories with examples of what kinds of items they might contain.

    • Watch
    • Movies, TV Shows, Documentaries, Web Videos, etc...
    • Read
    • Book, Article, Magazine, Blog Post, Email
    • Listen
    • Song, Album, Artist, Podcast, Talk
    • Learn
    • Subject, Skill, Sport, Language
    • Play
    • Vide Game, Board Game, Sport, Squid Game?

Customize Details

Depending on the category, Someday will try to provide a few details about the item you may wish to add. But you can always edit certain fields afterward.
